From Gen Z Distractions to Fateful Encounters

Written by the author of this website, Tyler V. (BraveCat)




Unshakable Perceptions


 Since before I graduated highschool in 2017 I shared a similar feeling many have, something isn't right. The education system is faulty, and to such a degree that we end up spending our early adult lives relearning and retraining our minds to adapt in a world with many secrets and caveats. By 2026 many "conspiracy" theories turned out to be true, others fabricated by agencies like the CIA, and anyone with access to the internet is bombarded daily with copious amounts of conflicting information..



 Prying our eyes open to the possibilities and truths is not a comfortable or trivial matter, but I found purpose in going down rabbit holes and yearning to see the world from a grand perspective. I had to let discernment and intuition guide my attention at each dead end, whatever I was going to find was not going to be magically validated by peers or even accepted within friend or family circles. What happened in the past, where does the future lead? Everything feels exponentially changing in the world; technology, social reform, weather patterns, even cosmological changes. Before anyone believes what our institutions want us to believe about our changing world, I implore anyone to think for themself and explore independent research to rule against institutional dogmas.


Gen Z Overload


 Growing up in a newer generation has its eases and challenges. "Strong men create good times" as seen during the first world wars and abysmal depression periods that forced people to adapt in dire situations and carved a world that desired stability. "Good times create weak men" brings us into the edge of the 21st century with ease of access to food, water, and education. The birth of the internet, cozy tech jobs, and entertainment at the press of a button. "Weak men create bad times" and here we see the cycle has already caught up with us mere decades later with financial struggles, the middle class bottomed out, and more unknown variables moving forward than solid ground to stand on.


Too Many Coincidences


 I've tried to compare findings from all sorts of various corners of history, religion, fields of science, and political decision making. Previous philosophers like Plato that once talked about civilizations come and gone. The fall of the Roman Empire itself, and more niche topics following archeology down the megalithic steps of time itself. The ongoing debunking of how the Egyptian Pyramids were made, tablets and cuneiform languages that go back many more thousands of years than what mainstream sources dictate. Hundreds if not thousands of arctic expeditions that are held secret or placed in government documents for few eyes to see. Things are catching up, the NOAA Space Weather Prediction center has their own public model for geomagnetic declination as the North Pole sky rockets toward Siberia. All the while normies argue over human influence on the climate, the planets around us are changing too while humans blame each other for Earth's own anomalies.


Leading A Charge


 That leads me here, I created this website as a means to house these "Frontier Signals" in a public and informational way for others to start their own line of thinking, to broaden the horizons for others in an open ended way where they can make up their own mind about how the world really works. Access to live data collected from numerous sources. Peer reviewed science papers that often get buried under mountains of mainstream narrative or muddied agendas. Links and references to helpful resources whether you're an upcoming enthusiast or trying to prepare for what the patterns are hinting toward. I have a calling, a sense of duty, will you join the new frontier? The new age of Earth awaits.
