Privacy Policy

At Frontier Signals, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information on our website.

1. Information We Collect

Frontier Signals is a static website with no database or user account system. We do not actively collect personal information from visitors.

Server Logs

Our web server may automatically log standard web server data, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referrer URL
  • Date and time of access
  • Pages accessed

This data is used solely for:

  • Diagnosing server problems
  • Analyzing website usage patterns
  • Improving site performance

2. Information We Do Not Collect

  • We do not use cookies to track your behavior
  • We do not use third-party analytics tools that track individual users
  • We do not request personal information
  • We do not sell or share visitor data

3. Third-Party Content

This website may link to external websites and embed content from third parties. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external sites. Please review their privacy policies before providing any personal information.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all connections
  • Security headers to prevent common web attacks
  • Regular security updates and patches

5. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is nothing to access, modify, or delete. However, if you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact the site owner.

6. Cookie Policy

Frontier Signals does not set tracking cookies. Your browser may store minimal technical cookies for session management if applicable, but we do not use them for tracking purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact the site owner through the About page.

GDPR Compliance

For visitors in the European Union, Frontier Signals complies with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) by:

  • Minimizing data collection
  • Not tracking individual user behavior
  • Not selling or sharing personal data
  • Using HTTPS encryption

Your visit to Frontier Signals does not create a data processing relationship under GDPR, as we do not identify or track individuals.
